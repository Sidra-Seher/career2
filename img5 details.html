Infinix

Infinix Mobile is a Hong Kong-based[1] smartphone company founded in 2013 by Transsion Holdings.[2][3] Infinix mobile phones are manufactured in France, Bangladesh, Brazil, Korea, Hong Kong, China, India and Pakistan and are available in Asia and in about 30 countries in the Middle East and Africa, including Morocco, Bangladesh, Kenya, Nigeria, Egypt, Iran, Iraq,[4] Pakistan, Cameroon and Algeria. The company has research and development centres sprawling between France and Korea and designs its phones in France. Infinix Mobile became the first smartphone brand manufacturing in Pakistan.
